## 1. What changed from v2 (2025-10) to v3 (2026-04)

Every number in this paper has been re-derived from raw corpus files after a forensic audit found that the v2 preprocessing pickle (`after_v4_final.pkl.gz`) silently corrupted two control corpora (Arabic Bible verses all became "Genesis"; Ksucca historical chronicle was mixed with poetry-meter labels). Eleven of the paper's quantitative claims and two of five "closed" proof gaps have been revised. The full reconciliation is in `paper.md §10 Retractions` and `supplementary/FINDINGS_SCORECARD.md`.

On **clean** raw-file data, 17 of 29 testable paper claims survive at or above the paper-reported strength, 6 are directionally correct but weakened, and 6 are falsified/retracted.

## 3. Before uploading to arXiv

arXiv requires either a LaTeX source tarball or a ready PDF with supplementary. Since the paper is markdown:

### Option A (recommended): convert to PDF, upload PDF + ZIP of supplementary

```powershell
cd arxiv_submission
pandoc paper.md -o paper.pdf --pdf-engine=xelatex --mathjax `
  -V geometry:margin=1in -V mainfont="Times New Roman"
Compress-Archive -Path supplementary\* -DestinationPath supplementary.zip
```

### Option B: convert to LaTeX, upload LaTeX source

```powershell
pandoc paper.md -o paper.tex --standalone
# then manually polish paper.tex (add \documentclass, add bibliography command)
```

arXiv prefers Option B (source) because its server re-renders the PDF.

## 9. Naming disclosure

The paper's headline framework retains the name **Shannon–Aljamal** from v2 (following the Kolmogorov–Smirnov naming convention: framework inheritor + specific characteriser), but §5 retracts **joint sufficiency** of the five conditions. The surviving claim is: conditions S1 (Mahalanobis separation) and S5 (path minimality), together with a weakened S4 (bigram sufficiency), jointly characterise the Quranic corpus among the 2,570-unit Arabic control pool. S2 (channel orthogonality) and S3 (constrained entropy rank #1) are falsified at unit level. The name is retained with this qualification.
